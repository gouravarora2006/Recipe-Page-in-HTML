Maggi Racipe

Ingredients

  • Maggi
  • Sauce
  • Salt
  • Water

Instructions

  1. Bring water to a boil.
  2. Add Maggi to boiling water.
    • Add salt to taste.
    • Cook for 2 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Add sauce to a pan
  4. Mix cooked Maggi with sauce.
  5. Enjoy!

Note: "Add salt to taste." and "Cook for 2 minutes, stirring occasionally."
